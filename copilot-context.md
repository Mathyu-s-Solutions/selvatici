## Brand Overview

**I Selvatici** is a brand born in 2026 in Basilicata, Italy, from the meeting of Lucanian and Tuscan roots and a shared vision based on love for:
- land
- food
- manual knowledge
- local traditions
- community

The brand creates experiences that combine:
- personal wellbeing
- healthy food awareness
- manual learning
- sustainability
- local identity
- community growth

The operational headquarters is in **Castelgrande**, Basilicata, and the brand can operate throughout Italy.

---

## Brand Purpose

I Selvatici exists to rebuild the relationship between:
- people
- land
- food

It promotes:
- awareness
- wellbeing
- healthy food choices
- sustainable habits
- real shared experiences

Core message:
**The brand reconnects people with the earth, food, manual knowledge, and each other.**

---

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term vision is to create the **Cammino dei Selvatici**:
a distributed network of gardens, kitchens, territories, and communities connected by traditions, biodiversity, food culture, and shared wellbeing.

This network should allow participants to:
- exchange recipes
- share practical knowledge
- identify seed varieties
- identify pollinators
- build a conscious and active community

This concept can be presented as a future vision section on the website.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Connection with the land**  
   Promote biodiversity, seasonality, and respect for natural cycles.

2. **Sharing and community**  
   Build relationships through doing things together.

3. **Food awareness**  
   Encourage healthy, sustainable, and informed food choices.

### 1. Cooking Workshops
Hands-on workshops focused on:

- food education

- conviviality

Main ideas:

### 2. Horticultural Therapy Workshops
Hands-on workshops focused on:
- gardening
- caring for plants
- sensory stimulation
- patience
- responsibility
- stress reduction
- connection with nature
- intergenerational exchange

Main ideas:
- the land teaches patience
- care creates belonging
- nature supports wellbeing

### 3. Sewing Workshops
Hands-on workshops focused on:
- sewing
- practical skills
- creativity
- sustainability
- wool transformation
- rediscovery of forgotten knowledge

Important detail:
participants learn the process of wool, from raw sheared wool to thread and finished garment.

Main ideas:
- give value to discarded materials
- reduce waste
- build self-esteem through making
- transform raw matter into something meaningful

### 4. Personal Chef Service
A private and customized culinary experience for homes, hospitality spaces, or private events.

Includes:
- custom menu design
- seasonal ingredient selection
- cooking and plating
- dish presentation and storytelling
- final kitchen cleanup

Culinary identity:
- rooted in traditional Italian cuisine
- especially inspired by Southern and Central Italy
- enriched by travel, cultural encounters, and learned techniques

### 5. Experiential Food Tours
Immersive territory-based experiences for:
- tourist groups
- associations
- local communities
- experiential travelers

Can include:
- visits to local producers
- seasonal harvesting
- cooking classes
- guided tastings
- routes across gardens, nature, and local kitchens

## Content Writing Rules

When generating copy:
- prioritize warmth and clarity
- avoid overly technical language
- avoid corporate jargon
- write with a poetic but understandable tone
- keep paragraphs readable
- highlight emotions, people, land, care, and shared experiences

Do not write like:
- startup SaaS marketing
- luxury hotel copy
- corporate consulting website
- industrial food business

Write more like:
- editorial lifestyle brand
- community and territory project
- food and wellbeing initiative
- artisanal cultural experience
